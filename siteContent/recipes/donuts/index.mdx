## Ingredients
#### For the cinnamon sugar 
* 3/4 cups sugar (granulated or row cane)
* 1 tbsp cinnamon

#### For donuts
* 4 tbsp butter (salted or unsalted, room temp)
* 1/4 cup sugar (coconut sugar would work too)
* 1 large egg
* 1/2 cup sourdough starter discard (or sour cream, or plain greek yogurt)
* 1/8 cup milk
* 1/2 tsp vanilla
* 1 1/4 cups AP flour
* 1/2 tbsp baking powder
* 1/4 tsp salt
* 2 cups refined coconut oil (for frying)

## Method
* mix cinnamon sugar ingredients and set aside
* in medium bowl, use a hand mixer to cream the sugar and butter until light and fluffy
* add eggs and liquids, mix well
* add everything else, beat just until incorporated, don't over mix
* allow to sit for 2-3 minutes
* flour counter, dust with flour, roll dough to 3/4" thick
* use a round cutter 3 inches wide, small cutter for centers
* heat oil medium heat, 3 inches deep.  Oil should be 375f
* fry, 2 minutes one side, 1-2 minutes the other
* Toss in cinnamon sugar mix
