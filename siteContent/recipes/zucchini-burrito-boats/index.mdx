## Ingredients
* Zucchini, slice lengthwise and hollow out
* Salsa
* Black Beans
* Cooked Chicken or Tofu
* Cilantro
* Quinoa
* Corn
* Red Onion
* Cumin + Chili Powder
* Cheese.

## Method
* Mix up everything except cilantro and cheese in a pan over medium head.
* Add cilantro once it's warmed through
* Spoon into zucchini boats and top with cheese.
* Bake at 400f for 30 minutes.
