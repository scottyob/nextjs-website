## Ingredients
* 1 bunch of spinach
* 3-4 garlic cloves minced
* Zest of half a lemon

## Method
* Heat a bit of oil in pan
* put lemon zest & cloves in oil for ~30 seconds or until fragrent
* Add spinach, toss, and wilt with lid until golden green
